## UPDATE BELOW: Pre-Workshop Videos & Activities
This workshop is primarily hands-on practice with KIRI Engine in order to learn to use key features of the software. To participate fully please do the following **before the workshop**:


- Download KIRI Engine on your phone from the App store or Google Play store. (5 min)<br>

- OPTIONAL - Make an account. Please note that you can only do one 3D scan without an account but once you make one you have unlimited access to making 3D scans. You also do **not** need to get a paid account.
